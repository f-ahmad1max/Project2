Our Motivation For Collaboration:
Less 'me' / More 'we'!​
7train Murals Creates Artistic Opportunities To Re-build Pride Within Our Queens And Manhattan Neighborhoods
Through Strategic Collaborations With Local Organizations And Members Of Our Community.
Our Murals Have 7 Distinct Community Building Characteristics:

  1. ​Our Murals Are Site-specific Designs - These Are Not Random Pages From A Sketch Book. The Mural Location Itself Inspires A Tailored Concept.
    2. ​
  2. Our Murals Are Beautifying The 7train Daily Commute - Our Mission Is To Beautify Neighborhoods In And Around The 7train Subway System In Queens And Manhattan.
  3. Our Murals Are Environmentally Friendly - Oil Based Aerosols Are Unhealthy For The Environment And For Our Lungs. We Use Water-based Paints And Reusable Brushes.
  4. Our Murals Are Legally Made - We Respect The Property Of Others. We Work With Landlords And Tenants In Securing Permission For Our Works Of Art.
  5. Our Murals Are Painted 100% By Volunteers - Not Bad, Eh?
  6. Our Murals Are Maintained Year-round - We Repair Any Vandalized And Weathered Murals With The Same Original Colors.
  7. Our Murals Are Funded By Grants And Sponsorship - We Research, Write, And Apply For Grants To Create These Neighborhood Artworks.
